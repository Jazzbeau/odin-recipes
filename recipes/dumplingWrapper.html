Dumpling Wrappers

Description

A surprisingly quick and easy staple to many different cuisines around the world, and it can be whipped up using some basic pantry staples!

Ingredients

  • 250g All Purpose Flour
  • 130ml Water

Steps

  1. Add water to the flour and gently mix until there is no loose flour left. Leave to rest covered for 10-15 minutes
  2. Knead the combined mixture into a smooth dough ball and then leave to rest for a further 30 minutes.
  3. Form a loop / donut shape with the mixture, then divide this into four equal parts.
  4. Divide each section into seven to eight small pieces.
  5. Each piece can then be rolled into a disc to form the dumping wrapper.
